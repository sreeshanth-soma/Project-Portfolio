About proXpo

Empowering Innovation through Peer Review
Innovation thrives on collaboration and feedback. To foster a culture of academic exchange, we are developing "proXpo", a web-based platform designed to enable students to showcase their projects effectively while encouraging engagement from peers and professors.

Key Features

  • Showcase Projects: Upload images and write project summaries to create a digital project portfolio.
  • Feedback Mechanism: Facilitate constructive feedback through user comments and a rating system.
  • Collaborative Learning: Enable professors and peers to interact, share insights, and contribute to project improvement.
